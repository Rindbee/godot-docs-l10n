A control that displays a texture by keeping its corners intact, but tiling its edges and center.

Description
-----------

Also known as 9-slice panels, **NinePatchRect** produces clean panels of any size based on a small texture. To do so, it splits the texture in a 3√ó3 grid. When you scale the node, it tiles the texture's edges horizontally or vertically, tiles the center on both axes, and leaves the corners unchanged.
